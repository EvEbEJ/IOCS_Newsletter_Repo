How to share IOCS with your Friends

Are you wondering how to share the news about IOCS? It's simple! Just give your friends, family members, or anyone interested this link: https://evebej.github.io/IOCS_Newsletter_Repo/subscribe/! Provided in this page is a short, 1-minute Google Form that asks for your email and a few extra details. Optionally, you can give the link to our blog, https://talkiocs.blogspot.com/ or this link to our instagram page (@iocscreation): https://www.instagram.com/iocscreation/.
