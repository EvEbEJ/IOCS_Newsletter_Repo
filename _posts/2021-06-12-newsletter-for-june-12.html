    Assumptions about Creationism

    by Evan Jiang

    Assumption 1: God created every species in the creation account.

    This statement never shows up in the Bible. In fact, Genesis 1:24 says, “And God said, 'Let the earth bring forth living creatures according to their kinds— livestock and creeping things and beasts of the earth according to their kinds.' And it was so.” Notice it does not say ‘species’ but ‘kinds.’

    ‘Species’ is a vague term. A human gets to decide whether a group of animals is similar enough to be a species, or if many are required. Some species are even based off the evolutionary timeline! Here’s just an example of how corrupted the definition of ‘species’ is. The domestic dog is grouped under the scientific name, Canis lupus. Some wolves are also grouped under this. However, coyotes fit into another species. So wolves and dogs are the same species, but coyotes are not.

    Back to kinds–The Bible says that God created each animal to its kind. What’s a biblical kind? Simply put, it’s a distinct group of animals. To expand this definition a bit further, kinds refer to the boundary of variation within an organism. For example, cats and dogs are different kinds. A cat cannot change into a dog, and vice versa. They are two distinct, separate kinds.

    God gives us all the answers in the Bible, his word. All we have to do is to take a look!

    The "Exploring the Fossils” column will contain a monthly article about fossils. June's fossil:


    Orthoceras

    Underwater Wonders

    by Ebenezer Jiang

    Like many other fossils, the name “orthoceras” may sound millions of years old, but in reality, these sea creatures dwelt only 6000 years ago. Despite the name, orthoceras were just like modern squids and octopi, only that they, unfortunately, tumbled into extinction. Orthoceras were squid-like organisms that dwelt in a narrow and pointed shell, like a snail.

    In an effort to demote Creationism, Evolution states that orthoceras are over 400 million years old.1 They lived on earth for 200 million years before they became extinct.2 Evolution also “masterfully” insists that orthoceras were the ancestor of modern squids.

    But how can earth, much less a single orthocera, be 400 million years old? And why did they evolve out of their shells to become squids? After all, a shell for modern squids would be extremely helpful in defense and protecting their soft bodies, and it may also provide camouflage. Additionally, 200 million years is a very long time for any animal to live on the earth. Scientifically, all of these monstrous dates are really just assumptions and words, not scientific facts.

    In reality and contrary to what Evolution instructs, Creation teaches that orthoceras were made on the fifth day of the creation week, not the millions of years that Evolution so desperately requires. In Genesis 1:20-21, the Bible tells us this:

    “And God said, Let the waters bring forth abundantly the moving creature that hath life, and fowl that may fly above the earth in the open firmament of heaven. And God created great whales, and every living creature that moveth, which the waters brought forth abundantly, after their kind, and every winged fowl after his kind: and God saw that it was good.”

    Not only were fish, sea reptiles, and pinnipeds created, but every kind of marine creature; this would include the orthocera. It is explicit and clear; orthoceras are not products of Evolution and a big bang that happened out of total nothingness. Not at all! Rather, orthoceras were fashioned by the world’s Intelligent Designer, showing the power of our Creator God, the great majesty of his work, and the perfectness of his Word.

    Evolutionists might say in defense, “Well, a fossil is required to be over 10,000 years old, so in order for orthoceras to be a real fossil, it must be 10,000 years or more.”

    Really? Where is the evidence for this corrupted assumption?

    No official record (other than evolutionary texts and evolution-biased study books) makes this statement!

    The English Oxford Dictionary simply defines fossils as “the parts of a dead animal or a plant that have become hard and turned into rock.” This doesn’t mean: “the parts of a dead animal or a plant that have become hard and turned into a rock that is at least ten thousand years old.”

    As long as something is the remains of an organism, in the form of rock or another substance, it is a fossil. There is no scientific evidence that requires a fossil to be over 10,000 years old. All fossils, from plants to animals, including orthoceras, do not have to be (and likely are not) 10,000 years old.

    Revelations 4:11 (KJV): “Thou art worthy, O Lord, to receive glory and honour and power: for thou hast created all things, and for thy pleasure they are and were created.”

    It Couldn’t Last That Long

    by Christian Conti

    The column, "Dinosaurs of Eden," is a bi-weekly article. This week's article:

    What happens to a chicken bone that gets left outside in the yard? Most of the time a dog or cat or other animal eats it or buries it in the ground. But what would happen if it were buried very quickly? It might turn into a chicken bone fossil. When it is fossilized, it will be completely replaced by minerals, and become rock solid. So, if you dug the chicken bone up, it would be a very hard, rocky replica.

    This process is common with dinosaur bones. Most scientists who believe in evolution will tell you that fossils from dinosaurs are over 60 million years old. But that simply cannot be true. Fairly recently, something happened that disproves the idea that dinosaur fossils are millions of years old. Scientists uncovered a fossil from a Tyrannosaurus Rex. But when they broke the fossil open, it still had soft tissues. They had not been replaced by rocky minerals. It is obviously not possible for soft tissue to last millions of years even if it were buried underground. It would have decayed or had been fossilized.

    The excuse scientists come up for this is that it “somehow” lasted 65 million years. But that is an outright farce. This helps us see that dinosaurs are not millions, but thousands of years old, and were created by a single God.

    The Roly Poly

    by Ebenezer Jiang

    Above: This roly poly is demonstrating the ability to roll up. Photo by Ebenezer Jiang.

    Roly Poly. Hmm, that sounds funny. Indeed, the Roly Poly (Armadillidium vulgare)1 (also called the pillbug or the sowbug) is a cool isopod, with a funny name. These critters, the size of a pill (about 18 millimeters long (that is almost 2 cm)), breathe through gills.2 This being the case, pill bugs need a moist climate in order to survive. Aren’t sow bugs pests? Yes, but even pests could have an amazing ability.

    ‘What is its ability?’ You ask. Well, great question. The Roly Poly’s ability sounds exactly like its name. They can roll up. Similar to an armadillo, roly-polies can roll up in for defense. Pillbugs have horizontal curved plates running along their bodies. These plates are so intricately designed, that they form a perfect sphere. With their soft bodies on the inside and an impenetrable shield, forming a locked defense system. Their hard shells provide great protection against almost every predator (only the fearsome Woodlouse Spider and a few others could get past such superpowers).

    Indeed, these pill-sized bugs, the size of less than 2 centimeters, clearly display the intelligent design of our God. “I will praise thee, O Lord my God, with all my heart: and I will glorify thy name for evermore.”3 How true these words are when we observe and consider the marvelous pillbug (until it is a pest), and every other animal for that matter. Let us praise and exalt the name of our benevolent God.
