    The Survivors of the 5 Mass Extinctions

    by Ebenezer Jiang

    Introduction

    Evolution readily teaches 5 mass extinctions. Today we are going to talk about two creatures, dating back some 480 million years,1 that have survived all 5 mass extinctions: the sea lily (Crinoidea) and the sea star (Asteroidea). This doesn’t appear to be plausible, does it? That’s right, it doesn't. We shall see why.

    Ordovician-Silurian Extinction

    The oldest mass extinction, the Ordovician-Silurian extinction, took place around 445 million years ago to 415 million years ago. During this period, 85% of all life supposedly died on earth. This implies that only 15% of all life during the first mass extinction survived. The Ordovician-Silurian extinction is said to be the second greatest mass extinction on earth. This extinction is suggested to be caused by toxic metals including iron, arsenic, and lead released on the earth, posing a threat to all marine life. This would also put sea stars and sea lilies at risk. Yet even according to National Geographic, evidence is lacking for the Ordovician-Silurian extinction.2

    Devonian Extinction

    Evolution claims that the Devonian extinction took place around 383 million years ago and lasted 20 million years. During this period, it is said that 75% of all life on earth, meaning only 25% of life survived. Somehow, both of the echinoderms, sea stars and sea lilies, are still hanging in there. The Devonian extinction is said to have occurred as a result of volcanic activities. Greenhouse gases such as sulfur dioxide could have contributed, as well as asteroid compacts. One compact, the Siljan crater in Sweden, is 32 miles wide. Occurring 377 million years ago, this crash into the earth is also credited to the Devonian extinction. Still, these dates and the blame of the Devonian extinction are not credible, and could be, and probably are false labels.

    Permian-Triassic Extinction

    Then, 252 million years ago, Evolution says the Permian-Triassic extinction took place. This is claimed to be the greatest mass extinction of all time. It is said that 96% of all marine life perished. Could sea stars and sea lilies really make that 4%? The Siberian Traps, found in Siberia, were a huge volcanic complex, which spewed out 720,000 cubic miles of lava. This eruption is assumedly credited to a large portion of the Permian-Triassic extinction. The seawater supposedly rose to 104 degrees Fahrenheit! Sea stars and sea lilies must have undergone massive adaptations to match Evolution’s expectations. Some features of the Permian-Triassic extinction could be slightly realistic, but the dates are complete fiction.

    Cretaceous-Paleogene Extinction

    The Cretaceous-Paleogene extinction is said to be the most recent mass extinction. Taking place 66 million years ago, about 76% of all life died. This 24% must still include the sea star and the sea lily. It is said that an asteroid seven and a half miles wide crashed into the earth, leaving a crater 120 miles wide in what is now the Yucatan Peninsula. Contrary to the other mass extinctions, global cooling actually occurred as a result of the dust from the compact. We do have evidence for the crater in the Yucatan Peninsula, which is now called the Chicxulub crater. But the compact made by the crater’s owner is exaggerated by Evolution.3

    Out of the mist, Sea Lily and Sea Star?

    Out of these 5 mass extinctions, we see that a great majority of life supposedly dies. Now, it’s time to get mathematical. In the Ordovician-Silurian extinction (445 - 415 mya), it is claimed that 85% of life died, meaning only 15% survived. In the Devonian extinction (383 mya), supposedly 75% of life was cleared, which means 25% remained. In the Permian-Triassic extinction (252 mya), it is presumed that 96% of life perished, implying that only 4% eluded extinction! In the Triassic-Jurassic extinction (201 mya), 80% of life is said to have fallen, telling us only 20% was spared. In the Cretaceous-Paleogene extinction (66 mya), 76% of all life was proposed to be eliminated by natural selection, which leads to 24%, which escaped death. And supposedly, both the sea star and sea lily survived all five mass extinctions! This is improbable. The chance of this happening for even one of these creatures escaping these mass extinctions is only 0.072%! Then, if we pile onto that, the fact that both sea lilies and sea stars passed through the wrath of these science-called extinctions, the probability is zero. Clearly, these monstrous dates and time periods are not mathematical and are incredulously unscientific.

    The Truth & Conclusion

    In reality, backed up by scientific evidence, there were never five mass extinctions. Evolution’s way of clearing the earth of its inhabitants is clearly an unscientific philosophy of deceit. From a Biblical perspective, it is also evident that all creation only existed for a few thousand years. This would include earth, the universe, and all that God made. Sea lilies and sea stars have always been sea lilies and sea stars since God set them on the earth. Genesis 1:24 says:

    “And God said, “Let the earth bring forth living creatures according to their kinds—livestock and creeping things and beasts of the earth according to their kinds.” And it was so.”4

    What’s the Deal With Carbon Dating?

    by Ellie Strano

    If you have been involved in Evolution discourse at any point in time, you have probably heard the phrase ‘Carbon Dating’ thrown around a couple times. This article will tackle what Carbon Dating is, its reliability, and why it is so important to evolution discussions.

    Carbon dating is a method scientists use to predict the age of a dead organism. Here is how it works: When an organism is alive, it consumes carbon. Most of this carbon is carbon-12, which has a stable amount of neutrons. However, some carbon is an unstable isotope of carbon made from nitrogen-14 in the upper atmosphere. This isotope is called Carbon-14. When an organism dies, it stops consuming carbon, and carbon-14 decays back into nitrogen-14 by emitting beta particles. Scientists measure the number of beta particles emitted and calculate, using the assumed rate of carbon-14’s decay, the age of the organisms.

    Of course, just from reading that summary many caveats and loopholes are visible. Carbon-14 dating doesn’t work on mussels or any other organism that hasn’t been exposed to carbon, because their beta radiation levels when freshly killed are at the levels of trees that have been dead for centuries. Radiation levels in coal, oil, and other gasses are so low, their Carbon-14 levels are also not reliable. However, one organism remains accurately dated by carbon dating: Trees. But, even some tree readings have been proven false, because the fundamental premise of carbon-14 dating is flawed.

    Carbon dating is predicated on the assumption that the rate of carbon decay has remained constant over the years - which is untrue. The amount of carbon-14 in the atmosphere fluctuates throughout the years. Fossil fuels and nuclear bomb tests also skew carbon dating results. As scientists study how carbon decay works, they re-evaluate carbon-dating discoveries. For instance, Siberia’s earliest human fossils have been shown to be at least 1,000 years younger than originally thought.

    The most important part of this article has to do with what carbon dating has to do with evolution. As many readers of this article will know, the Bible teaches that the Earth was made in six days. Although some Christians interpret this passage of Genesis as a metaphor and not literal teaching, all Christians can agree that God created each living creature intelligently and deliberately. The Theory of Evolution would argue otherwise. The Geological Time Scale, supported by Evolution, dates the earth back to 4.67 billion years ago when life first began. A few million years later, Archaea and protists form, and Oxygen from photosynthesis is first released into the air. After the Cambrian explosion, an era where scientists date the majority of invertebrate fossils, all the rest of living creatures evolve from these primitive, billion-year-old creatures.

    The problem is not that the process took billions of years, or even that carbon dating is used to extrapolate this evidence. The problem arises when carbon dating is used to justify the Darwinian idea that all of creation evolved from a single organism.

    Evolutionists explain that the Theory of Evolution does not explain the origin of life, and therefore Creationists and Evolutionists should co-exist peacefully. However, they simplify their own theory. The Theory of Evolution states that all organisms evolved from a single life force. It posits that all living creatures were created by means of natural selection, rather than intelligently by a Designer. The Theory of Evolution does not merely introduce the concepts of natural selection and survival of the fittest, it assumes that the world began from a single life force. They don’t know who created the life force, but they believe that it existed before anything else did, and created every living thing we know today.

    So what really is the problem with carbon dating? Nothing. It’s a process that dates fossils back to their origin with varying success. Like science, religion, and anything else, the problem arises when scientists rely on one tool when they really should try another.

    The Squirrel

    by Josiah Belli and Ebenezer Jiang

    Above: A scrounging squirrel was caught eating. Photo by Peter Goh.

    They're everywhere. Literally.1 While Squirrels (Sciuridae)2,3,4 are considered by many as pests, they certainly are one of God’s most fascinating and agile creatures. They are recognized for their long and bushy tails, which are, in fact, almost half their body length!5 They can effortlessly leap from tree to tree, often playing with others and hunting on the ground. Squirrels dwell everywhere - in forests, cities, and suburbs - any place with a tree and food is a place for a squirrel. Their nests (dreys) are constructed by a variety of artifacts, including twigs, leaves, and plastic bags.

    The squirrel is indeed a wonderful animal in many ways, but perhaps the most unique characteristic of the squirrel is its importance to the environment. As well as pests, squirrels are definitely wonderful gardeners too. Eastern Gray Squirrels are notorious for burying acorns. Each fall, these 1-pound squirrels dig holes in the ground to store up food for the winter. Squirrels embed almost 10,000 acorns in the ground each fall, that's burying about 110 acorns a day! Clearly, if the Ants and the Grasshopper had a squirrel inside, it'll be on the side of the Ants. Despite their bright mind and busy schedule over the fall, they forget up to 74% of the nuts they buried.6 This may be a bummer to the hard-working squirrels, but without squirrels, many of the oak trees around the US would not exist.

    Above: Nom. Nom. Nom. Photo by Daniel Goh.

    Incredibly, the Sciuridae is not just another Sciuridae. It is a wonderful creature fashioned by God. Created on the sixth day of Creation, these seemingly dull animals display in vivid ways the wonder and perfectness of God’s masterpieces, until they devise a master plan of how to rob the contents of your birdfeeder.
