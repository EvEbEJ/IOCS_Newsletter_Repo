    Evolution is Inconsistent

    by Evan Jiang

    Inconsistent? You ask.

    Yes. Here’s why:

    Evolution claims to be scientific, but it breaks scientific laws. The Law of Biogenesis (bī’•ō•je•nə•səs) says that life can only come from life. Clearly, evolution contradicts that when it says that life evolved from lifeless molecules. Also, the First Law of Thermodynamics (thər•mō•dī•na’•miks) states that “energy cannot be created or destroyed.” How about during the Big Bang? Where did that first energy originate?

    Evolution is not based on scientific, historical, or logical facts. People may gain absolute knowledge in many ways. Through science, you may utilize direct observation or experimentation. OK, let’s see if evolution can be proven with direct observation. First, we need a span of millions (or at least tens of thousands) of years. Now we need a person or a chain of people who lived throughout that entire span. They each need to observe the natural world and evolution. Easy, isn’t it? No. Experimentation itself is closely tied with observation. The data gained from experiments is obtained through observation. So if observation cannot prove evolution, then experimentation can't either. Historical fact depends on first-account witnesses or those who wrote down the first-account testimonies. No person ever recorded evolution. Logical fact depends on mathematical or logical deduction. Obviously, this has no application in terms of evolution. Therefore, evolution is not founded on fact.

    Evolution is based on orderly, predictable processes. Radio-isotope dating measures the decay of certain element isotopes, starting at the presupposition that the decay rate stayed constant. Erosion supposedly occurred at a steady pace. However, take a look at the Cambrian Explosion, when a great number of animals “appeared” in the fossil record. This is extremely sudden. In addition to the explosion, there are also many explosive extinctions. In fact, evolution says that there have been 5 major mass extinctions!1 Also, if evolution was true, then we would see at least a few hundred mutations with a gain of information. In contrast, we see none. Some evolutionists claim that this happens since evolution is unpredictable. It happened quickly back then. Now it’s “invisible.” Note the irony. Evolutionists use dating that employs constant time to prove a mechanism that is inconstant and sudden.

    Creationists, however, are neither inconsistent nor uncertain. Moses recorded God's first account of creation. Creationists also have one foundation that they hold on to in everything: the Bible, which is full of consistent truths.

    Sea Stars

    From Small to Large

    by Ebenezer Jiang

    Sea stars, or starfish, are without a doubt some of the most fabulous marine creatures on the planet. Sea stars, found in the family Asteroidea, are echinoderms. Sea stars are carnivorous with a unique method of hunting. These invertebrates come in all shapes and sizes, and so do their fossils.1

    Because of their great fame, Evolution has a lot to say about these echinoderms. According to Evolution, sea stars have been around 500 million years, since the Cambrian period, and have survived the five mass extinctions. Fossils from species Cantabrigiaster are one of the suggested transitions between crinoids (sea lilies) and sea stars.

    Unfortunately, this philosophy is critically erred. First, evidence exhibits the complexity of sea stars even during the Cambrian, 500 million years ago! Where is scientific evidence for the transitional ancestors for sea stars? Scientists think that sea star ancestors were fragile, lacking a calcite skeleton, and so did not fossilize. But this excuse is invalid, because we have found jellyfish fossils, which are fragile creatures. What about the Cantabrigiaster? The fact is not all Evolutionary sources agree on the Cantabrigiaster. According to the University of Western Australia, the evolution of sea stars and brittle stars are still a mystery.2 Cantabrigiaster was probably just a sea star. As sad as it seems, the fact is, there is no scientific evidence for sea stars or sea lilies evolving from anything or gaining complexity! Additionally, how did sea stars’ evolutionary status remain unchanged for 480 million years? All evidence and science shows us that sea stars were always sea stars. How could sea stars survive all five mass extinctions? How could the earth be that old? Evolution added a needless, sound-like-science library of sea star variations to lure people to trust in its doctrine.

    God tells us that He created all marine creatures on the fifth day. And there is no fault in God or His word. What science says supports a Creationist’s worldview. God created all animals as they were. Genesis 1:24 says:

    “And God said, “Let the land produce living creatures according to their kinds: the livestock, the creatures that move along the ground, and the wild animals, each according to its kind.” And it was so.”(NIV)

    From the moment creation began, each creature reproduced after their kind. This could only happen because of not the wondrous works of nature, chance, and evolution, but because of the wondrous works of God, the Intelligent Designer.3

    Spinosaurus

    by Ebenezer Jiang

    Spinosaurus - Sail-backed

    The Spinosaurus1 (“spined reptile”) has a vivid sail back. This feature is created from tall vertical spines. Instead of being for temperature regulation, this “sail” could have been in fact a hump to store water and lipids (like a camel). It could also have acted as a rudder in water or have been for display.

    The first fossils were found in 1915 by German paleontologist Ernst Stromer in western Egypt. Unfortunately, these fossils were bombed in World War II along with the museum in which they were kept. This dinosaur, although said to be some 100 million years old, is really only the age of a few thousand years. Spinosaurus is the largest known carnivorous dinosaur. Weighing the monstrous mass of 13 to 22 tons (12,000 - 20,000 kg), it measures 14-18 meters long, its skull alone being almost 2 meters! The Spinosaurus was specially designed by God for piscivory.2 Fossils reveal partially digested fish and their scales. The teeth of Spinosaurus have been discovered in pterosaur fossils. All the more reason for quick burial and the Spinosaurus’s menu.

    Science versus the Bible

    by Ellie Strano

    Which is more reliable, science or the Bible? Wait! Before we answer that, we have to understand a few things.

    First, what is science? Science is defined as a process used to solve problems or develop an understanding of natural events. The keyword here is ‘process’. Science is not a book of facts constantly being revised and updated; science is a thorough and precise way of obtaining evidence concerning the natural world.1

    Another important thing to remember is that science tends to agree with Christianity. For example, the Goldilocks Zone is a term scientists use to describe that Earth is in the exact right spot for life.2 If the earth were a smidge farther from the sun or a smidge closer, there would be zero life on Earth. As another example, neutrons and protons, two fundamental parts of every atom, are at the perfect ratio to sustain life. If the weight of neutrons were just a little bit lighter, or protons just a smidge heavier, the universe wouldn’t exist. This, as well as many other reasons, is why scientists characterize our universe as the ‘fine-tuned universe’.3 Scientists are constantly discovering new things about how perfect our world is, how the Earth was specifically designed for human life. The scientific process is based on the assumption that the universe is ordered and lawful. You literally can not be a scientist if you do not assume that there are laws in the universe that we can discover, a deeply Christian idea. To put it in Albert Einstein’s words: “The most Incomprehensible thing about the universe is that it is comprehensible”. 4

    Now that you know all this information: Which is more reliable, science or the Bible? It’s a trick question. Both science and the Bible are ways to understand God: Science is a process that helps us learn about God’s creation, and the Bible is the Word of God. The two were never at odds. So why would that question even be asked?

    The answer is quite simple. When in an argument, it would be an automatic win to frame your opponent as someone who is not factually based or someone who ‘doesn’t believe in science.'' If you are having a conversation with an atheist and they ask you this question, they are baiting you into a trap: They want you to say ‘the Bible’, so they can say that Christians aren’t grounded in the real world. The question is set up for the answerer to commit a logical fallacy because if you believe that God created the world, but you don’t believe in science, you have automatically lost the argument. Whichever way you answer the question ‘which is more reliable, science or the Bible?’, you are conceding that science and the Bible are contradictory. If you answer science, then that implies that the Bible (the word of God) isn’t as reliable as man-made discoveries, but if you answer the Bible, you imply that science contradicts the Bible, or is inadequate in some other way. Both science and the Bible are designed to be reliable sources of truth. By choosing one over the other you concede that one of them is faulty.

    I know what you are thinking, “What should I say when someone asks me, ‘which is more reliable, the science or the Bible?’” The obvious answer is to stop engaging in discussion with that person, for they clearly are not actually seeking truth and are just looking to cross-examine you on your faith. However, if you’re feeling especially evangelical, you could reply along the lines of, “Is it possible to believe in only one? How can you believe science without acknowledging that many scientific discoveries point to a lawful and ordered universe with a Creator?” God created the universe. Science is a vessel for understanding God’s creation in the same way that the Bible is a tool to understand God Himself. When you defend your faith, remember the universe and its Creator is on your side.

    The Sundew

    by Evan Jiang

    Above: Can you find the sundew in the picture? Photo by Daniel Goh.

    Take a look closely at the photo above. Can you see the deadly insect-eater amidst the moss? If you can’t find it, I’ll give you a clue: it’s not that small. (If you still can’t find it, read this article!)

    Sundews1, 2 (genus Drosera) are a kind of carnivorous plant. Their “fur” (tentacles) is tipped with beads of tantalizing nectar -- often fatal to the customer. A fly or other small insect will land on these tentacles to get a taste. However, they often end up being the ones being tasted. The insect becomes trapped by the sticky treat. Then the leaf curls around it, engulfing it in more tentacles. Eventually, the poor fellow gets smothered.

    You might be thinking, What a gruesome plant! It may seem that way to you. But we can still appreciate the wonderful strokes God has painted onto this creature! The sundew dwells mainly in nutrient-poor soil. God has provided sundews -- plants -- with a unique habit to obtain all that it needs. How true was Jesus when he said, “But if God so clothes the grass of the field, which today is alive and tomorrow is thrown into the oven, will he not much more clothe you, O you of little faith?”

    We do not need to worry. God will answer our needs according to His sovereign will.
