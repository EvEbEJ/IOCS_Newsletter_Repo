    Is the Bible Reliable?

    How reliable is the Bible? Does it have errors and inconsistencies? It is, after all, 2000+ years old. Discover the answer to this widely-debated question when you read the article!

    Link: https://iocsmin.wixsite.com/iocs/post/is-the-bible-reliable

    Many people think that the Bible contains many errors and contradictions. It is, after all, thousands of years old. Wouldn't such a book of antiquity be outdated and hold many scientific and historic inaccuracies and mistakes? The Bible tells us that God is 100% truthful — there is no deceit or lies found in Him (Numbers 23:19, 1 Samuel 15:29, Hebrews 6:18). 2 Timothy 3:16 says that "all Scripture is given by inspiration of God" (KJV). So if the Bible has errors, contradictions, and mistakes, then God is a liar. Either the Bible is 100% truthful and God is Who He says He is, or God is a liar and we are just beings with no purpose or meaning to life.
