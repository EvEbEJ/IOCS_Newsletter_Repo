This is the Pink Lady's Slipper, also known as the Mocassin Flower. Perhaps the most striking part of this temperate orchid is the largest, brightly-colored pedal (known as the labellum), contrasting the dull brown of the dead forest ground. Like this flower, Christians are called to be lights to the world (Matthew 5:16) because we have received eternal life from the Life-giver that the world has not seen. Let us be like this flower, like lights to this world.
