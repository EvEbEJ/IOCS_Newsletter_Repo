    The Age of the Earth

    4.5 billion years ago...or was it 6000 years ago? Does all of this really matter? Can't we just be satisfied saying: "A time not too long ago, but not too recently the heavens and earth were created...?" Find out!

    Link: https://iocsmin.wixsite.com/iocs/post/the-age-of-the-earth

    The age of the earth is a fundamental concept to establish correctly. Different conclusions will lead to different explanations of origins, observations in science, and Scripture. If we conclude with long-age, such as the 4.5 billion years that secular models propose, we can fit in theories that a shorter time slot would not allow (e.g. macroevolution).
