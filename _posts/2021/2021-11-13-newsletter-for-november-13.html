    In Perfect Balance: People - Truly Blessed

So water, when it evaporates from the skin, brings along with it tons of heat, thus relieving the distressed victim. 1

How does this unique phenomenon occur? Amazingly, the sun is 400 times the moon’s size, but ‘just happens’ to be 400 times as far away! What a compassionate God who desires for us to delight in the majestic awe of his creation! 2

    3. And a lot more...

      God certainly created everything in perfect balance in our everyday lives. In fact, everything the past two articles in this series expounded is an example of God's blessings to us! He is surely deserving of all praise and thanks!

    Coral

    A Bit At a Time

    by Ebenezer Jiang

    What is the most famous coral reef? Many people would say “The Great Barrier Reef” of Australia. Coral reefs are fascinating. Although it may seem that coral are plants, they are actually living organisms. Each member of the reef community makes up the beauty and size of these massive reefs. There are over 1000 species of coral. Some of which dwell in warm, tropical areas, while others take residence in the depths of the frigid ocean.1 Coral reefs are also vital for some marine life. Coral reefs are found in less than 1% of the ocean, and yet over 25% of all marine life lives there!2 It is clear and evident. Coral is important. But oceans are not the only places coral is found. Is it possible that there are corals in rock?

    Yep! It actually is. Coral is also found in rocks as fossils. Evolution teaches that all fossils were buried slowly, with occasional catastrophic activities. Corals, and coral reefs, according to Evolution, first appeared in the oceans about 500 million years ago. Then, they disappeared from the fossil record 350 million years ago. After another long 100 million years, they reappeared in the Middle Triassic Period, or 240 million years ago (about the same time as the introduction of dinosaurs).3,4

    It seems a little confusing, right? And it is, because Evolution messed up. (Evolutionists definitely messed up on the simplicity of coral.) All of these hundreds of millions of years are unscientific. No one can observe it, and so it falls short of the requirements of the scientific method. Also, hundreds of millions of years is a long time. 100 million years is one with eight zeros (100,000,000 years). Additionally, wouldn’t coral decay before it ever got a chance to fossilize? Remember, not only would there be bacteria, but also other marine creatures such as scavenging fish (such as hagfish) and worms, which would delight to nibble on a piece of coral lying around. Also, why is there a gap in the fossil record? What Evolution tries to excuse as a mass extinction is in reality something else…

    Creation teaches something drastically different. Not quite a million years ago, but only about 6000 years ago, God did something awesome. He created the entire universe in one week. The Bible says that God created marine creatures on the fifth day. This would include coral. The fossils of coral were formed in one event, the Flood. For such organisms to be found in fossils, a quick burial is required. The Flood provided just the thing. The gap in the fossil record for Creation? Unlike Evolution, which uses the fossil record as a timeline, Creation recognizes that the Flood’s catastrophe altered the fossil record greatly. Also, many columns and walls of fossilized coral reefs where there is no water around, conspicuously point to a Flood.

    It is evident from the corals in the oceans, to the fossilized ones found in rock; all point to an Intelligent Designer. Corals along with every other creature proclaims the might and glory of God. So, let us also proclaim the greatness of our God, following the words of Psalm 66:1-2:

    “Shout for joy to God, all the earth; sing the glory of his name; give to him glorious praise!” (ESV)

    Pachycephalosaurus

    by Ebenezer Jiang

    Pachycephalosaurus - science is changing

    Pachycephalosaurus (pronounced: patch - e - sef - foe - lo - `sore - us) is known for its uniquely shaped head. Many interpret this dinosaur’s 10-inch thick skull as a weapon for ramming, like what goats do. But according to Britannica, Pachycephalosaurus’ head is for mere display.

    Pachycephalosaurus grows 6 to 7 feet tall. They were thought to be herbivorous, but they have teeth which many experts think support a carnivorous background. Another commonly accepted statement is that the Pachycephalosaurus is some 60 million years old. But along with all of the other doubtful interpretations, evidence shows that this philosophy is likely erred as well.     Science versus the Bible

    by Ellie Strano

    Which is more reliable, science or the Bible? Wait! Before we answer that, we have to understand a few things.

    First, what is science? Science is defined as a process used to solve problems or develop an understanding of natural events. The keyword here is ‘process’. Science is not a book of facts constantly being revised and updated; science is a thorough and precise way of obtaining evidence concerning the natural world.1

    Another important thing to remember is that science tends to agree with Christianity. For example, the Goldilocks Zone is a term scientists use to describe that Earth is in the exact right spot for life.2 If the earth were a smidge farther from the sun or a smidge closer, there would be zero life on Earth. As another example, neutrons and protons, two fundamental parts of every atom, are at the perfect ratio to sustain life. If the weight of neutrons were just a little bit lighter, or protons just a smidge heavier, the universe wouldn’t exist. This, as well as many other reasons, is why scientists characterize our universe as the ‘fine-tuned universe’.3 Scientists are constantly discovering new things about how perfect our world is, how the Earth was specifically designed for human life. The scientific process is based on the assumption that the universe is ordered and lawful. You literally can not be a scientist if you do not assume that there are laws in the universe that we can discover, a deeply Christian idea. To put it in Albert Einstein’s words: “The most Incomprehensible thing about the universe is that it is comprehensible”. 4

    Now that you know all this information: Which is more reliable, science or the Bible? It’s a trick question. Both science and the Bible are ways to understand God: Science is a process that helps us learn about God’s creation, and the Bible is the Word of God. The two were never at odds. So why would that question even be asked?

    The answer is quite simple. When in an argument, it would be an automatic win to frame your opponent as someone who is not factually based or someone who ‘doesn’t believe in science.'' If you are having a conversation with an atheist and they ask you this question, they are baiting you into a trap: They want you to say ‘the Bible’, so they can say that Christians aren’t grounded in the real world. The question is set up for the answerer to commit a logical fallacy because if you believe that God created the world, but you don’t believe in science, you have automatically lost the argument. Whichever way you answer the question ‘which is more reliable, science or the Bible?’, you are conceding that science and the Bible are contradictory. If you answer science, then that implies that the Bible (the word of God) isn’t as reliable as man-made discoveries, but if you answer the Bible, you imply that science contradicts the Bible, or is inadequate in some other way. Both science and the Bible are designed to be reliable sources of truth. By choosing one over the other you concede that one of them is faulty.

    I know what you are thinking, “What should I say when someone asks me, ‘which is more reliable, the science or the Bible?’” The obvious answer is to stop engaging in discussion with that person, for they clearly are not actually seeking truth and are just looking to cross-examine you on your faith. However, if you’re feeling especially evangelical, you could reply along the lines of, “Is it possible to believe in only one? How can you believe science without acknowledging that many scientific discoveries point to a lawful and ordered universe with a Creator?” God created the universe. Science is a vessel for understanding God’s creation in the same way that the Bible is a tool to understand God Himself. When you defend your faith, remember the universe and its Creator is on your side.

    The Herring Gull

    by Ebenezer Jiang

    Top: A Herring Gull glides over Salisbury, New Hampshire. Photo by Ebenezer Jiang.

    Bottom: Seagulls are found everywhere! This seagull sits lazily off the coast of Australia. Photo by Earnest Law.

    The ocean is full to the brim with creatures —— from the creepy, bottom-dwelling arachnids to the microscopic plankton that fills the ocean’s surface. But creatures also dwell on the coasts of the ocean. One of the most abundant of such creatures are seagulls. Seagulls are any kind of 40 species of seabird with webbed feet found in the family Laridae and in suborder Lari. These seabirds live worldwide.1

    WARNING: Seagulls have the capability to peck with their sharp beaks. Beware!


    Top: Seagulls are everywhere! Photo by Earnest Law.

    Bottom: A seagull stands pridefully on a rock. Photo by Ebenezer Jiang.

    Above: The carcass of a seagull's victim. Photo by Ebenezer Jiang.

    One of the most amazing things about seagulls is their intelligent way to hunt. Often, begging for food from beach visitors is not enough. Seagulls have to rely on the source of food that the ocean provides. The seagulls' diet includes fish, crustaceans, and mollusks. Other than stealing and being fed food, seagulls obtain an intelligent way of feasting that only an Intelligent Designer could have installed into their half-inch sized brain. We all know that although hard-shelled invertebrates such as crustaceans and mollusks are opulently delectable, they can be extremely difficult to pry open. God gave seagulls understanding as to exactly how to get these prey. Seagulls secure their crustacean or mollusk prey with their plier-like beaks. Then, calculating the height and ground surface, they release their victim while flying, leaving it to fall helplessly and splatter onto the hard ground below. Amazingly, this trick not only opens the prey but also provides a nutritious meal that doesn’t get all over the place. And all of this knowledge is stored in the unbelievable brain size of a pea.2,3

    Conspicuously, God is an Intelligent Designer. Ever since the fifth day, when gulls were fashioned, God’s fascinating design has echoed throughout creation. Let us praise Him, while not only looking out for seagulls that might rob us of our lunch, but also for clams or crabs that may hail from the sky at any moment.
