    by Evan Jiang

    Science, true science, is totally based on science. In contrast, historical science is assuming the past; it is composed of guesses (accurate or not) based on evidence that we have. In other words, historical science is faith.

Therefore, the Flood and Creation all are historical science. However, unlike what its name implies, historical science is not history. History is made of eyewitness accounts, not a mere assumption. Here is where the Creation account differs from evolution. Inside the Bible, God’s holy word, we are given a precise, eyewitness account of the creation of the world.

    Trilobites

    Tripling Bites On Evolution1

    by Ebenezer Jiang

    Trilobite. When many people hear this word, they immediately shrug it off as some random extinct organism, but the trilobite has more to offer than just an extinct critter. The word “trilobite” means “three lobes.” If you say it slowly, notice how much it sounds like “tri-lobe-ite.” (“Tri-” for triple (or three), “lobe” for lobe, and bites, because then it sounds like an organism.)2 Most, if not everyone, who know about trilobites, confirm that they are extinct. (You don’t just go to the beach and find them anymore.) Well, how did they become extinct? This is where Evolution and Creation begin to battle. Which is right? Which is wrong?

    Evolution insists that these exotic crustaceans dwelt on the earth in the Paleozoic era, that is 250 to 570 million years ago. How were they fossilized? ‘Simple!’ Evolutionists would state, ‘Over a few million or tens of thousands of years, and over slow, constant burial. Eventually, the trilobite would be totally submerged in an air-proof matter and fossilization would occur.’ See a problem with these monolithic assumptions?

    Trilobites are like roly-polies (check out our roly-poly article HERE!); they have the amazing ability to curl up into a tight ball when frightened. And we have observed many fossils of these marine creatures in such compacted and curled up poses. Now, when trilobites die, they uncurl because, similar to a clam, the muscle that holds them in a ball relaxes after death. Okay, so why do we see trilobites rolled up? If this were the case, they would have to be alive. Even if Evolution says that a few “lucky ones” got buried when in this pose, this argument is extremely invalid because we don’t just observe a few, but many such specimens of curled-up trilobites!! What is the chance of that happening? Another obstacle is tissue. How can we find intact tissue in trilobite fossils that lived over 100 million years ago and were buried in a way that would kill the tissue? There is no reasonable explanation for how such tissue could remain for such a bizarre duration of time. And this is only the surface. Many factors about the trilobite bite into Evolutionary ideas. Obviously, Evolution doesn’t seem to be very secure. Perhaps there is a better solution to the “trilobotic” dilemma.

    Above: A trilobite of the genus Flexicalymene.

    Creationism stands with a probability that is above and beyond the zenith of the probability of Evolution’s explanation. Creationism teaches that the world is about 6000 to 7000 years old. If this is the case, trilobites would most certainly not be millions of years old. The Flood probably occurred 4000 to 5000 years ago. Now, the Flood would have quickly buried life. The catastrophism, the earth’s tectonic movement, and all of the movement and water would have instantaneously frozen creatures in place. Now, this could be the case with trilobites. The crustaceans, frightened from the flood’s presence, would instinctively roll up into a ball. This would explain why we find fossils of curled-up trilobites since the trilobites were buried alive and would not be uncurled. Also, the tissue would only have to last at most 5000 years. It is obvious, there is no more need for millions and gazillions of years.

    In Job 36:26, Elihu (Job’s friend) says, “Behold, God is great, and we know him not, neither can the number of his years be searched out.” Indeed, how true these words are! God is so great. Even the itty-bitty trilobites, which He fashioned, points to His great workmanship. Isn’t it amazing that our God can do what Evolution would need billions of years for, in less than a few thousand years? (And even less if he wanted to!) How great is our God! Let us magnify His name with the words from Psalm 8:1, which says, “O Lord, our Lord, how excellent is thy name in all the earth! who hast set thy glory above the heavens.”

    Ambulocetus

    by Christian Conti

    Above: An artist's interpretation of the supposed missing link Ambulocetus

    Supposed “intermediate links” have rarely been found in the fossil record. If the earth was in fact millions and billions of years old, we would have millions of intermediate links in all the billions of years the earth would have been around. One of the major gaps or “missing links” is that between land mammals and whales. In comparison to most other mammals, whales are built for a very different and specialized way of life. For such a change to occur in an animal is out of the question, but evolutionists have to consider it to fit their beliefs.

    Eventually, scientists discovered a supposed link between another extinct creature, Mesonyx, and whales. They gave it the name Pakicetus. It was a big deal for scientists and the time. Where did they get their evidence that it was anything whale-like? Absolutely no part of this creature was anything like that of marine mammals. What we do have of this creature indicates that it was solely terrestrial.

    However, evolutionists did not give up in their desperate search. They had a bigger, more important find. This discovery was that of Ambulocetus (walking whale). Ambulocetus was carnivorous, and probably an amphibious mammal similar to mustelids. It grew to about 11 to 12 feet and weighed over 400 pounds. It lived around Pakistan, along with Pakicetus. This creature could apparently walk on land and swim in the water. It could have been semi-aquatic, similar to an otter. But it certainly was not a whale. Why? Simple logic; its body structure, appearance, and way of life were nothing like that of cetaceans.

    The Prairie Dog

    by Evan Jiang

    Above: A lone prairie dog stands outside its burrow. Photo by Daniel Goh.

    What prairie animal is a keystone species, a species on which many plants and animals depend for survival? If you said prairie dogs, you are correct. Prairie Dogs (genus Cynomys) are residents of the Great Plains in North America. Their numbers once reached the millions, but their population has long since dwindled. This decline has been attributed to settlers, who drastically wiped out prairie dogs due to misinformation and faulty assumptions.

    WARNING: Prairie dogs will bite, and many are capable of spreading disease.


    Prairie dogs live underground in massive networks of tunnels and burrows known as towns. These towns can reach gigantic sizes, with the largest on record (from Texas) coming in at 25,00 square miles!1 To put this into scale, the state of West Virginia is around 24,000 square miles. Prairie dogs have an incredible role in the environment. They trim grass and other plants, which creates a ‘cycle’ of various plants that attract larger herbivores. Their burrows make excellent homes for animals such as rattlesnakes and burrowing owls. Prairie dogs also serve as a hearty meal for their predators, which come in a variety of shapes and sizes, from hawks and coyotes to rattlesnakes and the endangered black-footed ferret, which only feeds on prairie dogs. In addition to constructing towns and being food, prairie dogs have a complex language to warn other residents of approaching danger. According to the National Park Service, prairie dogs are capable of reporting a predator’s size, color, and more!2

    Although they are an iconic species, prairie dogs display much more than fame. They show God’s intricate design, and are truly the ‘work of God’s hands.’
