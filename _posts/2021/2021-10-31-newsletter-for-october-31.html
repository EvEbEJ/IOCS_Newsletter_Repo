    In Perfect Balance: Carbon

    by Evan Jiang

    More than 90% of all known compounds have carbon. It is used in literally every part of our bodies, from amino acids to cell membranes. So what’s the big deal with this element?

    1. Carbon is the only element that can form strong structures with other carbon atoms.

      Each carbon atom is capable of creating four separate bonds with four other carbon atoms. These bonds are very stable, thus allowing carbon to create long chains. In addition, carbon-based molecules are not dismantled when dissolved by the “universal solvent,” water. This is extremely vital to our lives, as many of the nutrients and basically all the vitamins our bodies need are carbon-based molecules. 1, 2

    2. Carbon aids in vision.

      Carbon’s unique ability to form multiple bonds with other atoms is crucial to sight. It is used as the base for long molecules that absorb the different wavelengths of light, thus giving our lives not only light but also the blessing of color.1

    Ammonites

    Evolution - Spiraling in Circles

    by Ebenezer Jiang

    If you went to the beach, what is the first thing you think you will see? It may be seagulls, perhaps a crab or two, and maybe some fish. Well, one marine creature that was abundant before the great flood was the notable ammonite. Ammonites have a shell similar to that of a snail which spirals away. Like the orthocera (Check out our orthocera article HERE!), these shells were the hospitable home of a unique cephalopod (Cephalopods include octopi and squid and a generous assortment of other similar animals). These magnificent marine creatures varied in size; they could be one inch or nine feet long! Ammonites, who at first abundantly dotted the ocean, now abundantly dot rocky slabs of fossils. How’d these fossils form? You know, I’d really like to know!

    “Oh, simple.” A secular scientist would tell you. “Starting from the Jurassic period, ammonites have roamed the earth for 130 million years. At the end of the Cretaceous, that is 66 million years ago, they eventually became extinct.”1

    Millions of years? I don’t know about that. It is evident through the genealogies in the Bible that the earth is probably only about 6000 years old. (You may find yourself going in circles if you try to refute the Bible.) Also, when we look up the fossil record, it’s surprising what we find (if we look through “Evolution’s glasses”). Throughout this so-assumed “130 million years,” we find no major evolution going on, even though in 130 million years, they should get more complicated. All we find is more elaborate shell patterns on what are called “evolved” ammonites.2 In fossil-embedded sheets of clay that are up to 100 feet tall, how is it that we have not the faintest glimpse of ammonites evolving? After all, these 100 feet are millions of years of bit-by-bit fossilization according to the secularist. It’s all clear and explicit. There were no millions of years and no macroevolution in ammonites (and anything else for that matter), and that topples the Evolutionary theory.

    The answers are simple for the Creationist. About 4000 to 5000 years ago, a worldwide flood engulfed the earth. High levels of catastrophic movement in the earth would have caused great turbulence and sediment erosion, which could have quickly buried the ammonites. These buried ammonites would have become the beautiful and elaborate fossils that we view on display today. Also, interestingly, a study was done on the Chambered Nautilus, which are similar to ammonites. Scientists discovered that these mollusks take a long time to mature enough to breed and reproduce. If this was also so with the ammonite, and the maturing time period was longer than the Chambered Nautilus, floodwaters could have decimated the ammonite population, and brought them down to extinction. Ammonites truly display God’s splendor and the truthfulness of His word. These fabulously designed creatures show not only Intelligent Design but also show the error of Evolution. So the next time you go on a walk, or go perhaps to the beach, remember that every creature that you come across is specially designed by God, and praise Him for it. Who knows? That creature may hold a secret waiting to be unlocked.

    Iguanodon

    by Evan Jiang

    Iguanodon - 2nd dino

    Iguanodons - sound like iguanas right? You are correct. Iguanodon, named for its iguana-like teeth, was a herbivorous dinosaur that excavators discovered in flood rocks that evolutionists date as Cretaceous. Its hand displays a distinct spike for a thumb.1

    Iguanodons have quite a history. They were the 2nd dinosaur to be discovered. They also have a lengthy record of misconstruction (i.e. scientists messed up its appearance). For example, Gideon Mantell, who discovered the iguanodon, placed the spike-thumb on the Iguanodon’s nose! In fact, sculptures of this supposed Iguanodon were displayed in London’s Crystal palace!1,2

    1. https://www.britannica.com/animal/Iguanodon
    2. How do we know the thumb was not originally a horn? Because complete (as far as "complete" goes in paleontology) fossil skeletons have been excavated.

    Science versus the Bible

    by Ellie Strano

    Which is more reliable, science or the Bible? Wait! Before we answer that, we have to understand a few things.

    First, what is science? Science is defined as a process used to solve problems or develop an understanding of natural events. The keyword here is ‘process’. Science is not a book of facts constantly being revised and updated; science is a thorough and precise way of obtaining evidence concerning the natural world.1

    Another important thing to remember is that science tends to agree with Christianity. For example, the Goldilocks Zone is a term scientists use to describe that Earth is in the exact right spot for life.2 If the earth were a smidge farther from the sun or a smidge closer, there would be zero life on Earth. As another example, neutrons and protons, two fundamental parts of every atom, are at the perfect ratio to sustain life. If the weight of neutrons were just a little bit lighter, or protons just a smidge heavier, the universe wouldn’t exist. This, as well as many other reasons, is why scientists characterize our universe as the ‘fine-tuned universe’.3 Scientists are constantly discovering new things about how perfect our world is, how the Earth was specifically designed for human life. The scientific process is based on the assumption that the universe is ordered and lawful. You literally can not be a scientist if you do not assume that there are laws in the universe that we can discover, a deeply Christian idea. To put it in Albert Einstein’s words: “The most Incomprehensible thing about the universe is that it is comprehensible”. 4

    Now that you know all this information: Which is more reliable, science or the Bible? It’s a trick question. Both science and the Bible are ways to understand God: Science is a process that helps us learn about God’s creation, and the Bible is the Word of God. The two were never at odds. So why would that question even be asked?

    The answer is quite simple. When in an argument, it would be an automatic win to frame your opponent as someone who is not factually based or someone who ‘doesn’t believe in science.'' If you are having a conversation with an atheist and they ask you this question, they are baiting you into a trap: They want you to say ‘the Bible’, so they can say that Christians aren’t grounded in the real world. The question is set up for the answerer to commit a logical fallacy because if you believe that God created the world, but you don’t believe in science, you have automatically lost the argument. Whichever way you answer the question ‘which is more reliable, science or the Bible?’, you are conceding that science and the Bible are contradictory. If you answer science, then that implies that the Bible (the word of God) isn’t as reliable as man-made discoveries, but if you answer the Bible, you imply that science contradicts the Bible, or is inadequate in some other way. Both science and the Bible are designed to be reliable sources of truth. By choosing one over the other you concede that one of them is faulty.

    I know what you are thinking, “What should I say when someone asks me, ‘which is more reliable, the science or the Bible?’” The obvious answer is to stop engaging in discussion with that person, for they clearly are not actually seeking truth and are just looking to cross-examine you on your faith. However, if you’re feeling especially evangelical, you could reply along the lines of, “Is it possible to believe in only one? How can you believe science without acknowledging that many scientific discoveries point to a lawful and ordered universe with a Creator?” God created the universe. Science is a vessel for understanding God’s creation in the same way that the Bible is a tool to understand God Himself. When you defend your faith, remember the universe and its Creator is on your side.

    The Orb-Weaver

    by Ebenezer Jiang

    Top: An orb-weaving spider - This species makes a bold and distinct line of silk down its web. Photo by Ebenezer J.
    Bottom: This spider, a brown commoner, is not as colorful nor as large. Photo by Ebenezer J.

    “And God saw everything that he had made, and behold, it was very good. And there was evening and there was morning, the sixth day.”1 In the Bible, we read that everything in creation was very good. But what about the spiders? Not many people these days regard spiders as a five-star favorite. Why are spiders so creepy and why do their webs always get everywhere? Spiders are actually more complicated and Intelligently Designed than they seem. Orb-weaver spiders (family Araneidae) happen to fall into this category. Orb-weavers build their webs in bushes, meadows, woods, and even under porches.

    WARNING: Orb-weavers are non-venomous. But they will bite when they feel threatened. Their painful bites can leave itchy wounds. Be cautious when you are in Orb-weaver territory.


    Above: These unfortunate honeybees somehow wound up as hot lunch. Photo by Daniel G.

    Orb-weavers are renowned for their large, orb-shaped webs. These masterful weavers are explicitly Intelligently Designed. Attached to the spinneret are glands that can produce different kinds of silk protein fibroin. Because of this, these spiders can produce threads that vary in size, stickiness, and flexibility. All of the different glands are crammed into the weaver’s abdomen. The webs too have purposeful design. Threads, called mooring threads, connect to a stable surface. Radial and framing threads connect the mooring threads to make a sturdy outline. Spiralling out from the radial threads are viscid threads. The orb-weaver’s webs are massive; some can build webs that reach the diameter of 3 feet (36 inches, ~1 meter)! While patiently waiting for food, the orb-weaver perches on the radial threads. When an insect flies into the meter-long trap, the spider can detect the intensity and direction of the insect’s desperate vibrations from its perfect location.

    Conspicuously, the orb-weaver is Intelligently Designed. From the complex spinneret to the web structure and stability, spiders are obviously amazing arachnids. So the next time you see a creature that may look disgusting, before you say “Eew!!,” remember that everything is Intelligently Designed, and perhaps, has a secret waiting to be unlocked.
